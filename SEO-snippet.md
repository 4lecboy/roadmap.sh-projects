### Head snippet (SEO, OG, Favicon)
```html
<head>
  <meta charset="utf-8" />
  <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1" />
  <title>Your Name – Frontend Developer</title>

  <!-- SEO -->
  <meta name="description" content="Short summary of your experience and focus (50–160 chars)." />
  <meta name="robots" content="index, follow" />
  <link rel="canonical" href="https://your-domain.com/cv" />

  <!-- Open Graph -->
  <meta property="og:title" content="Your Name – Frontend Developer" />
  <meta property="og:description" content="Same or slightly shorter than the description." />
  <meta property="og:type" content="website" />
  <meta property="og:url" content="https://your-domain.com/cv" />
  <meta property="og:image" content="https://your-domain.com/og-image.jpg" />

  <!-- Favicon -->
  <link rel="icon" href="./favicon.ico" sizes="any" />
  <!-- or PNG/SVG -->
  <!-- <link rel="icon" type="image/png" href="./favicon.png" /> -->
</head>
```
